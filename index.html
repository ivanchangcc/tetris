TETRIS

Stack tetrominoes, clear lines, and chase your high score.

How it works

  • Rotate and move pieces to form full horizontal lines.
  • Completed lines clear and you score points.
  • The game ends when the stack reaches the top.

Controls

←→ Move · ↑ Rotate · ↓ Soft drop · Enter Start · R Restart (after game over)
